Security Questions

Common questions about how we protect your family

How do you protect my payment information?

We never store your payment information on our servers. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor used by millions of businesses worldwide. Your credit card details are tokenized and encrypted at every step.

What happens if there's a security breach?

While we've never experienced a breach, we have protocols in place. We would immediately notify affected users, provide detailed information about what occurred, and offer free credit monitoring if necessary. Our incident response team trains quarterly for such scenarios.

Can TimeBank Kids employees see my family's data?

No. Our zero-knowledge architecture means your data is encrypted before it reaches our servers. Even our engineers cannot decrypt or view your personal information. Only aggregated, anonymized data is used for improving our service.

How often do you update your security measures?

Security is an ongoing process. We perform daily automated security scans, weekly manual reviews, monthly penetration testing, and quarterly third-party audits. Our security team stays current with the latest threats and continuously improves our defenses.

Found a Security Issue?

We take security reports seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure. If you've discovered a vulnerability, please let us know immediately.

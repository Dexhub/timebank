Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service effectively:

Parent Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Device information for app access
  • Communication preferences

Child Account Information

  • First name and age
  • Task completion data
  • Screen time usage patterns
  • Achievement and reward history

What We DON'T Collect

  • Children's last names or full identities
  • Precise location data
  • Contents of children's devices
  • Social media information
  • Biometric data

How We Use Your Data

Every piece of data we collect serves a specific purpose in helping your family:

Data Type Purpose Who Can Access
Task completion Track progress and calculate rewards Parent accounts only
Screen time usage Enforce limits and provide insights Parent accounts only
Email address Account security and updates TimeBank Kids support (when needed)
Age information Age-appropriate task suggestions Automated system only

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security audits every 6 months
  • Data Isolation: Each family's data is logically separated
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on SOC 2 compliant servers

COPPA Compliance

TimeBank Kids is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This means we:

  • Never collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Provide parents full control over their children's data
  • Allow parents to review and delete their children's information at any time
  • Never sell or share children's data with third parties
  • Implement industry-leading security measures to protect children's privacy

COPPA Safe Harbor

TimeBank Kids participates in the PRIVO COPPA Safe Harbor certification program. Our certification can be verified at privacy.privo.com

Your Rights as a Parent

You have complete control over your family's data:

Access Rights

  • View all data we have about your family
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • See a log of who has accessed your data

Control Rights

  • Update or correct any information
  • Delete specific data points
  • Close your account and delete all data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

Communication Rights

  • Choose which emails you receive
  • Control notification preferences
  • Opt out of product research

Data Sharing Policy

We NEVER sell your family's data. Period.

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: Only when you explicitly approve
  • For security: To prevent fraud or abuse
  • Legal requirements: If required by law (we'll notify you unless prohibited)
  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help run our service (under strict contracts)

Security Measures

Technical Security

  • 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Intrusion detection systems

Operational Security

  • Employee background checks
  • Security training programs
  • Limited data access
  • Regular security audits

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Backups: Removed from backups within 90 days
  • Legal holds: Only retained if legally required

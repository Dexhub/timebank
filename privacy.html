Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At TimeBank Kids, protecting your family's privacy is our top priority. We believe that your personal information belongs to you, and we're committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect it.

Information We Collect

  • ✓ Account information (name, email, password)
  • ✓ Child profiles (first name, age)
  • ✓ Usage data (tasks completed, time earned)
  • ✓ Device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • • Provide and improve the TimeBank Kids service
  • • Send important account updates
  • • Provide customer support
  • • Ensure safety and security

What We Never Do

  • ❌ We NEVER sell your data
  • ❌ We NEVER share personal information with advertisers
  • ❌ We NEVER track children across apps or websites
  • ❌ We NEVER use your data for targeted advertising

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✓ Access your data anytime
  • ✓ Update or correct information
  • ✓ Delete your account and all associated data
  • ✓ Export your data in a readable format

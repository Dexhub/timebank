Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TimeBank Kids

How is this different from regular parental controls? ‚ñº

Traditional parental controls are restrictive and often lead to conflicts. TimeBank Kids is empowering - it teaches kids to manage their own time by earning it through positive behaviors. Instead of just blocking access, we're teaching valuable life skills like time management, work ethic, and financial literacy.

What ages is TimeBank Kids suitable for? ‚ñº

TimeBank Kids works best for children ages 6-17. The system adapts to different age groups with appropriate tasks, earning rates, and features. Younger kids get simpler tasks and visual rewards, while teens can access more complex financial education features.

How do you prevent kids from gaming the system? ‚ñº

Parents approve completed tasks before time is credited. We use smart verification methods like photo uploads for certain tasks and location verification for outdoor activities. The system also tracks patterns to flag unusual behavior. Most importantly, the positive reinforcement model naturally encourages honest participation.

What devices does it work with? ‚ñº

TimeBank Kids works with all major platforms: iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, and popular gaming consoles. Our app integrates with device-level controls to enforce time limits across all connected devices. One account manages everything.

What happens if my child runs out of screen time? ‚ñº

When time runs out, devices enter "earning mode" - kids can see available tasks to earn more time. Emergency override options are available for parents. The key is that kids learn the consequence naturally and are motivated to plan better next time, rather than just being cut off.

Our Story

In 2022, our founder Sarah Chen was at her wit's end. Her three kids were constantly battling over screen time, and every day ended in tears and frustration.

"I tried everything," Sarah recalls. "Timers, charts, apps that just blocked access. Nothing worked long-term. My kids saw screens as forbidden fruit, and I was always the bad guy."

Then came the breakthrough: What if kids could earn their screen time like adults earn money? What if we could teach them that time has value, effort brings rewards, and they have control over their choices?

TimeBank Kids was born. Today, we're helping thousands of families transform their relationship with technology, one earned minute at a time.

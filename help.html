Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started" and follow our 5-minute setup wizard. You'll add family members, set up tasks, and configure screen time rules.

How do I add my children?

From your parent dashboard, click "Add Child" and enter their first name and age. No personal information required!

Troubleshooting

My child can't log in

Children don't have separate logins. They access TimeBank Kids through your parent account on authorized devices.

Screen time isn't being tracked

Make sure you've installed our companion app on your child's device and granted necessary permissions.

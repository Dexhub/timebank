Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use TimeBank Kids on multiple devices? +

Yes! TimeBank Kids works on all your family's devices. Parents can manage from any smartphone, tablet, or computer, and kids can access their accounts from their own devices. All data syncs automatically across devices.

What happens if my child completes extra tasks? +

Extra completed tasks earn bonus screen time that can be saved for later use. You can set maximum daily limits to ensure healthy boundaries, and unused time can roll over to weekends or special occasions based on your settings.

Can I customize screen time limits for different days? +

Absolutely! You can set different limits for weekdays, weekends, and even specific days. For example, you might allow more screen time on Saturdays or during school holidays. The system is fully customizable to match your family's schedule.

Is there a minimum age for children to use TimeBank Kids? +

TimeBank Kids is designed for children ages 4-17. For younger children (4-7), we recommend parent-assisted task completion. The app adapts to show age-appropriate interfaces and suggests suitable tasks based on your child's age.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel anytime from your account settings with no cancellation fees. Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period. Before cancelling, you can export all your data for your records.

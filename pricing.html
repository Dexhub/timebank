Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our plans and pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there really no credit card required for the free plan?

Absolutely! The Starter plan is completely free forever with no credit card required. It's perfect for trying out TimeBank Kids with one child.

What happens after the 30-day trial?

After your trial ends, you'll be automatically charged for your chosen plan unless you cancel. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% off for educators and homeschooling families. We also have special pricing for schools and organizations. Contact us for details.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason, just contact us for a full refund.

Can I add more children later?

Of course! You can add children to your account anytime. If you exceed your plan's limit, we'll help you upgrade to the right plan for your family.
